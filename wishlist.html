Wishlist

We are looking forward to celebrating our wedding, and the greatest gift of all would be to enjoy it together with you! However, if you still think of spoiling us, we have prepared a list of wishes containing some items we could still use for our love nest.

Since the wedding takes place in Veracruz and we live in Bern, we figure that the easiest way to gift us with something would be by a donation of money for fulfilling one of our wishes.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

On the wishboard, you will find the list of items we would like to have. You can select one and fulfill the "wish" completely or partially. After you register the wish, you will receive an email with the payment information. We, Brenda and Fedor, will buy the items after our honeymoon.

If you prefer, you could also send us physically a present to our address in Bern. If that present happens to be on our wishlist, please register for fulfilling the wish completely (all the parts of it) and write us an email at wedding@brenda-and-fedor.com.
We kindly ask you to avoid bringing big presents on the wedding day, since we can't take them back to Switzerland.
