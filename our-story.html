Our story

Growing up in different parts of the world our paths crossed first in a language school in Vichy France. During the 4 weeks together in classes, we noticed that we feel more for each other. Naively and in love we called us a couple form then on.

Things got more complicated from then on, Brenda went to study in Montpellier and Fedor had to join the military. But this did not hinder us from spending all the time together that we could. Brenda's stay in Montpellier was unfortunately limited to one year she had to go back to Mexico to finish her bachelor's studies. During this time, Fedor started his Bachelor's Studies in Computer Science. Every holiday we reunited in Mexico or in Switzerland.

After 3 years of living in distance, Brenda came to Switzerland to enter a Master's studies. During this time Fedor proposed to Brenda and got the most wonderfully answer he could wish for.
A couple of months later, Brenda repeated her "yes" at their civil marriage in Bern. Now after so much time, we finally are getting married by church in Mexico.

Looking back it almost seems impossible that we meet each other and stay together even with this big distance separating us.
Overall the key to our story is our love for God. Without him, we would not have the strength and the endurance to keep our relationship going.
